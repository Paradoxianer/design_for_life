# DFL App – Design for Life

The **DFL App** is a digital companion designed to support the _Design for Life_ (DFL) weekend seminar.  
Its purpose is to simplify preparation, centralize materials, digitize questionnaires, and assist leaders—
while preserving the deeply personal, spiritual, and relational character that defines DFL.

---

## 🌟 Purpose & Vision

The DFL App enhances the in-person seminar experience without replacing it.  
It provides participants and leaders with digital tools that make the process smoother, clearer, and more accessible.

**Key principles:**
- Digital **support**, not digital **replacement**
- Maintain the personal, spiritual atmosphere of DFL
- Reduce administrative workload and paper usage
- Make materials, questionnaires, and schedules easily accessible
- Provide leaders with helpful insights and preparation tools
- Support a multilingual, international audience
- Strictly comply with privacy and data protection standards (GDPR)

For the full vision document, see:  
➡ `docs/vision/vision.md`

## 👥 Target Users

The application is tailored to three primary user groups:

### **Participants**
- Fill in spiritual gifts and values assessments
- View seminar schedule
- Take notes
- Upload their Life Tree (image/drawing)
- Access teaching materials

### **Small Group Leaders**
- View participant lists
- Access test results and Life Tree uploads
- Prepare for 1:1 conversations
- Keep private notes

### **Administrators**
- Manage users and groups
- Upload materials
- Maintain translations/languages
- Configure seminar settings

## 🔐 Privacy & Data Protection

The DFL App handles spiritually sensitive personal data.  
Because of this, we follow a strict privacy-first approach:

- GDPR compliant
- EU-based servers
- Minimal data retention
- Encrypted communication and storage
- Roles & permissions for data access
- Clear data deletion policies
