# Acceptance Tests (UAT) – DFL App

## 1. Participant Journey: "The Weekend Experience"
**Goal:** A participant can navigate the entire DFL process digitally.

| Step | Action | Success Criteria |
|------|--------|------------------|
| 1 | Find & Register | Participant finds their weekend in the list and registers successfully. |
| 2 | Questionnaire | Participant completes the Gifts assessment; Top 3 are displayed. |
| 3 | Life Tree | Participant builds a tree with at least 5 branches and marks "Red Threads". |
| 4 | Key Takeaways | Participant enters at least one takeaway for the "Values" session. |
| 5 | Collage | Participant opens the Collage and sees all their takeaways pre-loaded. |
| 6 | SMART Goals | Participant defines 3 goals and all 5 SMART checks are green. |

## 2. Leader Journey: "Support & Guidance"
**Goal:** A group leader can effectively support their participants.

| Step | Action | Success Criteria |
|------|--------|------------------|
| 1 | Group Overview | Leader sees all assigned participants and their "Completion Badges". |
| 2 | Review Shared Data | Leader opens a participant's shared Life Tree summary and can discuss it. |
| 3 | Prayer Note | Leader sends a "Word of Encouragement" (Prayer Note) to a participant. |
| 4 | 1:1 Feedback | Leader documents a 1:1 talk and clicks "Share with Participant". |

## 3. Admin Journey: "Event Preparation"
**Goal:** An admin can set up a DFL weekend in under 15 minutes.

| Step | Action | Success Criteria |
|------|--------|------------------|
| 1 | Create Event | Admin creates "DFL Weekend Berlin" with location and dates. |
| 2 | Template Schedule | Admin loads the "Standard DFL Template" and adjusts 2 session times. |
| 3 | Room Setup | Admin defines "Main Hall" and "Group Room 1" and assigns them to sessions. |
| 4 | Grouping | Admin assigns 10 participants to 2 leaders using the drag-and-drop list. |

## 4. Privacy & Safety Check
**Goal:** Ensure no spiritual data is leaked.

| Step | Action | Success Criteria |
|------|--------|------------------|
| 1 | Presentation Check | Participant presents their Collage. The leader's device (viewer) shows NO private notes. |
| 2 | Unshare | Participant revokes sharing for their "Gifts". The leader's access is immediately revoked. |
| 3 | Admin Privacy | System admin tries to open a participant's prayer notes. Access is denied/Data is encrypted. |
